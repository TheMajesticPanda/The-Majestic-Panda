The Backstory

The Majestic Panda was created by Kelvin Lightner and Jenny Gonzalez.
This website was created based off Jenny's addiction for Pinterest and her love for blogs.
We came up with this product as the perfect combination of the two.

Jenny: Why did you choose this project?

My project reflects my interests because I love being creative and I have an obsession with Pinterest. I love the idea of people getting to express their knowledge of any art and any animation. My project is also interesting because my mentor is a software engineer and when I grow up I want to be a software engineer. I believe this will be a fun experience for me.

Kelvin: Why are you most excited for this project?

I have a passion for web development. I believe that the web is designed to connect people and allow people to express themselves. In other words, the web fosters connection. I am most excited to share my knowledge of fundamental web design ideas and equip another person with the skills to contribute positively to society.

Both: What is the impact you want to have on others through this website?

Pictures and words allow for humans to connect with each other and their pasts. It is not only important to make art but to also share it.
